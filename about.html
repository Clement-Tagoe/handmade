About Us

WHO WE ARE

Hand Made cosmetics is a cosmetic brand that belongs to you. We care about your skin and how to better care for it.

Over the years we have come to realize the amazing glow certain natural extracts has on our skin and we dare not keep that a secret, we share that with you through our products.
We provide you with the very best in skin care because to us, your skin represents you. We are not just passionate about what we do, we are intentional and purposeful , we are driven and fueled by the desire to let your kin stand out .
This is why we make sure that our products go through harsh testing and are made carefully with nothing but the best extracts, processed under very fine conditions and more importantly fit for purpose.

WHY CHOOSE HANDMADE COSMETICS

Over at hand made cosmetics, we are of the impression that you are your best self when you wear the skin you love, we are dedicated on making you love the skin you have. Our products go on to remove dead skin cells from the skin, leaving your skin clean.
Also, we make our products with vitamins and natural oil extracts that treats weak skin cells and nourishes the skin giving you the clear radiant skin you deserve. Our products come for all skin shades so we make sure you are getting the very best from us.
